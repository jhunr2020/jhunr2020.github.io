Population

                Its population as determined by the 2015 Census was 46,306. This represented 3.53% of the total population of Bohol province, or 0.63% of the overall population of the Central Visayas region. Based on these figures, the population density is computed at 193 inhabitants per square kilometer or 501 inhabitants per square mile.
